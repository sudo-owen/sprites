monchain

monchain is a onchain monster battling pvp game. players battle 1v1 with teams of mons in real-time. monchain is designed to bring multiple web3 communities together and showcase the power of extensibility of the EVM. under the hood, monchain is powered by CHOMP, a flexible battle engine that can support a wide variety of custom moves, effects, and mons.

game philosophy

though it obviously borrows a lot from pokemon, there are several distinct design choices for monchain:
  • focus on pvp over pve. this has several downstream consequences:
    • no grinding or leveling is planned to start, nor will there be an extensive single player compaign.
    • mons will be balanced periodically in an effort to keep the meta interesting. mons will not be tokenized to start.
  • mons as champs ala MOBAs vs mons with slottable moves:
    • more unique movesets / abilities
    • elemental typings are the high-level way to have counterplay
    • less mons, ideally make mons feel mechanically more unique

gameplay

both player take turns making moves. moves get executed, state advances. your mon might die. continue until all mons for one side are dead. you know the drill.
